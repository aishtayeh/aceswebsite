Mission and Services

Provide customers with solution to any standing engineering project. The solution
is designed to maintain sustainability of the engineering facility to provide the
reasonably expected services during its analysis period.
The solution includes consultations and quality control for materials of
construction, roadway construction, structural engineering and another civil
engineering works.

Fresh and hardened concrete tests:

Testing fresh Portland cement concrete for workability and density.

 Compressive strength of concrete.
Obtaining and testing drilled cores to

determine the strength of hardened
concrete. 

Construction material tests:

Proper laboratory testing of materials to determine their physical properties is an
integral part of the design, construction and quality control of any kind of
engineering structure. 

Stone, Tile, Concrete Block ... Tests:

Water absorption of natural stones
Determination of natural stone defects.
